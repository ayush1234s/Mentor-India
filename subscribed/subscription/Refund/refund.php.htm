Refund Policy

Please review our refund policy carefully before proceeding.

  • If you apply for a refund on the same day of purchase, the full amount will be refunded.
  • If you apply for a refund the next day, the refund amount will be processed after deducting 20% of the purchase price.
  • If you apply for a refund after the next day, 50% of the purchase amount will be deducted, and the remaining 50% will be refunded.
  • If you apply for a refund after the third day, no refund will be provided.

Note: If you purchase within 12 hours, the same-day refund applies. If you request a refund at 10 PM and the 12-hour window is not complete, the refund day starts from the next day, which means you receive the full amount if you apply the next day.

  • Go to your subscription course receipt.
  • At the end of the receipt, click on the "Refund Amount" button.
  • Fill out the refund form with the following details:
    • Account Holder Name
    • Account Number
    • IFSC Code
    • Branch
    • Purchased Courses
    • Purchased Course Date
    • Amount to be Refunded (shown after selecting course and dates)
    • Upload Signature (PNG format up to 50kb)
  • Submit the form by clicking on the "Apply" button.
